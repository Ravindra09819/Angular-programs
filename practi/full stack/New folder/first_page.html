10 places to visit in pune

Pune is a lovable city for various reasons. Dubbed the second major “IT hub of India” and the top “automobile manufacturer of India”, Pune has been often ranked as the most liveable city in India. Being a major city in the state of Maharashtra, Pune once served as the political hub of the Maratha Empire. Home to various historic and cultural monuments and structures, the city has witnessed the Mughal-Maratha Wars and the Anglo-Maratha Wars.

Pune is easily accessible from the other major cities in Maharashtra. Hence, tourists who seek to visit Mumbai also happen to include Pune in their itinerary. Pune experiences a delightful climate around the year and is most pleasant during the winter. Along with tourists, the city welcomes students and professionals from all parts of the country who migrate over here to pursue their careers. Go through this article to find the best places to visit in Pune. Nicknamed the “Oxford of the East”, Pune has a lot to offer for all sorts of travellers.
